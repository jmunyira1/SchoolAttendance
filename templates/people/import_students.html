How this works
  1. Select the Form / Grade the students belong to.
  2. Upload your Excel or CSV file — it must include a Stream column (e.g. Alpha, Beta).
  3. The system combines your selected form with the stream from the file (e.g. Form 3 + Alpha = Form 3 Alpha).
  4. Students are matched by Admission Number — existing records are updated, new ones are created.
